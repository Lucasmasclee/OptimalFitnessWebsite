Frequently Asked Questions

Can I customize the app for my gym?

Yes! We customize the app with your gym's branding, colors, and logo. Your members will see your gym's identity throughout the app experience.

What does it cost?

Pricing is based on your gym's size and member count. We offer flexible plans that scale with your business. Schedule a call to get a custom quote.

How quickly can we get started?

We can have your branded app ready in as little as 2 weeks. The onboarding process is simple and we provide full support to get your members started.

Is it only for beginners?

While we specialize in beginner-friendly content, the app includes plans for all fitness levels. Members can progress through different difficulty levels as they improve.

Does it work on iOS and Android?

Yes! The Optimal Fitness app is available on both iOS and Android devices. Your members can download it from the App Store or Google Play Store.
