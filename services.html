Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the partnership agreement?

Our partnership includes white-label app licensing, branding customization, member management tools, analytics dashboard, and ongoing technical support. We handle all the technology while you focus on your gym operations.

How do we handle member data and privacy?

We take data privacy seriously. All member data is encrypted, stored securely, and complies with GDPR and other privacy regulations. You maintain ownership of your member data, and we provide tools for data export and management.

Can we integrate with our existing gym management software?

Yes! We offer API integrations with popular gym management systems. For Enterprise clients, we can build custom integrations to work seamlessly with your existing software stack.

What happens if we exceed our member limit?

We'll work with you to upgrade your plan seamlessly. There's no interruption to service, and we'll help you transition to the next tier that fits your growing member base.

Do you provide marketing materials for promoting the app?

Absolutely! We provide marketing kits including social media templates, email campaigns, in-gym signage, and promotional videos to help you successfully launch and promote the app to your members.
