Questions About the App

How does this actually reduce member dropout?

The app eliminates the two biggest reasons members quit: confusion about what to do and lack of visible results. Guided workouts provide structure, while progress tracking shows measurable improvement. Members who see results and have clear guidance are 40% less likely to cancel.

What if our members don't want to use an app?

We provide proven marketing strategies that drive 85% member adoption. The key is positioning it as a value-add to their membership, not an additional cost. Most members appreciate having guidance and structure, especially beginners who are most likely to quit.

Does this replace our personal trainers?

No, it complements your existing services. The app handles basic guidance for members who can't afford or don't want personal training. This actually frees up your trainers to focus on higher-value clients and reduces the burden on your staff.

Can we track how our members use the app?

Yes! You get access to a dashboard showing member engagement, workout completion rates, and retention metrics. This helps you identify which members might need extra support and measure the app's impact on your business.

What equipment do members need?

The app includes workouts for all equipment levels - from bodyweight exercises to full gym equipment. Members can choose plans that match your gym's available equipment, ensuring they can complete workouts with what you have.
