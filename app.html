Frequently Asked Questions

Can we track how our members use the app?

Yes! You get access to a dashboard showing member engagement, workout completion rates, and retention metrics. This helps you understand which members might need extra support.

Do members need a personal trainer to use the app?

No, the app is designed to be completely self-guided with detailed instructions and video demonstrations. However, it can complement your existing personal training services.

What equipment do members need?

The app includes workouts for all equipment levels - from bodyweight exercises to full gym equipment. Members can choose plans that match your gym's available equipment.

Can members sync with fitness trackers?

Yes, the app integrates with popular fitness trackers and smartwatches to automatically sync workout data and provide more accurate progress tracking.

Is the app available on both iOS and Android?

Absolutely! The Optimal Fitness app is available on both iOS and Android devices, ensuring all your members can access it regardless of their phone preference.
